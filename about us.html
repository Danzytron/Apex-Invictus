ABOUT US

Team History

Founded in 2022 by a group of passionate Mobile Legends players, Apex Invictus emerged from the competitive scene with a singular vision: to dominate through innovative strategies and unwavering teamwork. What started as five friends grinding ranked matches has evolved into one of the most formidable esports organizations in the region.

Our journey began in local tournaments, where we quickly made our mark with innovative strategies and clutch performances. Within two years, we've climbed the ranks to compete in major championships, establishing ourselves as a force to be reckoned with in the Mobile Legends professional scene.

Our Vision

To be the premier esports team in Southeast Asia, known not only for our championship wins but also for setting the highest standards of sportsmanship, professionalism, and dedication within the gaming community.

OUR VALUES

The pillars that define our team's spirit and commitment to the esports community.

Teamwork

Collaboration and mutual support are the core of our strategy, ensuring seamless execution in every match.

Discipline

We maintain rigorous practice schedules and strategic preparation, essential for competitive longevity.

Innovation

Constantly developing new strategies and adapting to the evolving meta is key to staying ahead.
